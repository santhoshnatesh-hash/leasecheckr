Why This Matters

Car leasing is one of the most opaque purchases most consumers make. The monthly payment is what dealers show you. The money factor, residual value, and cap cost are what actually determine what you pay — and most people never see those numbers clearly.

LeaseCheckr is built to make that process more transparent. But a benchmark is only as good as the data behind it. Founding Members are the first verified contributors who help make that possible — by uploading a real NJ lease document, they help build the dataset that every driver who comes after them will benefit from.

In return, they receive included Premium access and early-member status as part of their Founding Member standing.

How It Works
01
Upload a verified NJ lease document
Submit a real NJ lease agreement — a signed contract, a dealer worksheet, or a complete quote document. Manual entry alone does not qualify. The document is what builds the benchmark.
All personal identifiers — name, address, VIN, contact info — are fully redacted before your document is processed.
02
Your submission is verified
We confirm the document contains the necessary deal data. Your personal information — name, address, VIN — is handled carefully and not stored in identifiable form in the benchmark.
03
Founding Member status is confirmed
Once verified, your Founding Member status is established and your contributor record is noted. Included Premium access applies to your account from that point forward, with no separate report fee required.
🔒
How We Handle Your Document
Personal identifiers are redacted before your document enters our system. What is retained is deal data only — payment, term, mileage, money factor, county. No name, address, contact information, or VIN is stored in identifiable form in the benchmark. For the complete account of how documents are handled, see our privacy policy.
Qualification Requirements

Manual form entry alone does not qualify, regardless of how complete the entry is
