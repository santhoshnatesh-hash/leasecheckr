Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This privacy policy explains how LeaseCheckr collects, uses, and protects information submitted through this platform.

Information We Collect

When you submit a lease quote, we collect the deal parameters you enter — including vehicle details, payment, term, mileage, and ZIP code. We do not require account creation to receive a free result.

If you choose to upload a lease document, we extract deal data from the document for benchmark purposes. Personal identifiers — including your name, address, phone number, and vehicle identification number — are fully redacted before any data is stored.

How We Use Your Information

Deal data submitted through the platform is used to generate your score and, where you have consented, to contribute to the anonymised NJ lease benchmark dataset. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Document Handling

Uploaded lease documents are processed for data extraction only. Personal identifiers are stripped from the document before any data enters our benchmark. Raw documents are not retained after processing is complete.

Cookies

We use cookies to support site functionality and to understand how visitors interact with the platform. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at privacy@leasecheckr.com.
