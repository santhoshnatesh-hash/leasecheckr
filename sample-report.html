💬 Exactly What to Say

Use these word-for-word at the dealership.

Opening — Get the number
"Before we finalize, can you show me the money factor? I'd like to see it written on the deal sheet."
Why: Signals you understand lease structure. Dealers give better terms to informed buyers.
Counter — Push for buy rate
"I know Honda Financial's base rate is around .00125 for this term. Is there any way to get closer to that?"
Why: Shows you know the buy rate without being aggressive. Gives them room to negotiate.
Closer — Use competition
"I have a similar offer from another Honda dealer. If you can match .00150, I'd prefer to work with you."
Why: Competition motivates movement. Dealers don't want to lose a deal they're close to closing.
F&I — Decline add-ons
"I appreciate the offer on the protection packages, but I'd like to finalize without any additional products."
Why: F&I offices make significant profit on add-ons. Declining upfront prevents pressure.
