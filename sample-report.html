💬 Questions to Ask Before You Sign
Screenshot These
These are the exact questions that separate informed NJ lease shoppers from everyone else. A good dealer will answer all of them without hesitation. A bad one will deflect.
"What is the money factor on this lease?"
If they say they don't know, or give you an APR instead, that's a red flag. You want the raw money factor. Multiply by 2,400 to convert to APR yourself.
"What is the residual value and where does it come from?"
It should match Honda Financial's published program. Dealers cannot change the residual — if it's different, ask why.
"Can I see the full cap cost breakdown?"
You want every line item: MSRP, dealer discount, rebates, trade-in, add-ons, acquisition fee, and any other adjustments. If they won't show you, walk out.
"Is the acquisition fee capitalized or due at signing?"
Honda's standard acquisition fee is $895. If it's capitalized, you're paying interest on it. Ask to pay it at signing instead.
"What happens if I go over mileage?"
Standard overage is $0.15–$0.25/mile on Honda. If you drive 12k/yr but signed for 10k, that's 7,200 miles over 3 years — up to $1,800 at signing return.
"Is GAP insurance included or am I being charged for it?"
Honda Financial leases include GAP coverage automatically. If a dealer is charging you for additional GAP, that's a pure add-on — decline it.
